## Design Context

### Users
SnapTrack is for calorie-conscious, health-conscious people who want to monitor intake for weight loss. They are usually checking the app in quick moments around meals, so the experience should prioritize fast scanning, low friction logging, and clear progress toward a daily calorie target.

### Brand Personality
Simple, clear, clean. The product should feel calm and trustworthy rather than intense or hyper-gamified. It should reduce mental overhead and make food logging feel straightforward.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a restrained, refined interface with strong readability, minimal clutter, and a polished sense of order. Visual decisions should support clarity first, with subtle emphasis for progress, actions, and status. Avoid busy decoration, noisy dashboards, or flashy wellness-app tropes.

### Design Principles
1. Make the primary action obvious and fast to reach.
2. Keep every screen easy to scan in under a few seconds.
3. Use restraint: fewer accents, fewer competing elements, stronger hierarchy.
4. Let data feel dependable through consistent formatting and spacing.
5. Preserve accessibility and touch comfort as part of the visual quality bar.
